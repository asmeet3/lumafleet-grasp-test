## **Welcome to Confluence!**

You can use Confluence to create a rich knowledge base or to collaborate with others using words, videos, images, graphics, charts, and graphs.

This page walks you through some Confluence basics like spaces, pages, and elements that allow you to create and manage impactful content for your teams.

## **Confluence 101**

<https://www.loom.com/share/5149671332234032989863f520d133ed>

### 🗃️ Spaces

Spaces are where teams organize ideas, projects, and documentation. Spaces can be customized and integrated with both Atlassian tools and others.

Spaces can be organized with [folders](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/use-folders-to-organize-your-work/), and you can drag and drop to reorder or nest content so you can more effectively share your work with others.

[More about spaces](https://www.atlassian.com/software/confluence/guides/get-started/set-up)

### 📄 Content types

**Live docs** let you collaborate in realtime with your team for better ideas, faster decisions, and unified edits – all without needing to publish.

**Pages** start as drafts that can be published into source-of-truth knowledge for your team — ideal for sharing policies, guides, and playbooks.

**Whiteboards** are your team’s place to brainstorm, build connections, and make ideas come to life.

**Databases** enable teams to build a library of information, track deliverables, and aggregate tools, links, and information from within and outside Confluence into a customizable view.

**Smart links** allow users to integrate and display content from other tools and platforms directly within Confluence, enhancing productivity by centralizing information in one accessible location.

[More about live docs](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/create-and-collaborate-in-real-time-with-live-docs/) | [More about pages](https://www.atlassian.com/software/confluence/guides/get-started/create-content) | [More about whiteboards](https://hello.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/~766660823/whiteboard/3078363456?atlOrigin=eyJpIjoiNGJkN2VmODk5NzMzNDZmYWE2N2RmN2ViYzBiZjgzZjciLCJwIjoiY29uZmx1ZW5jZS1jaGF0cy1pbnQifQ) | [More about databases](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/create-and-edit-databases/) | [More about smart links](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/bring-all-your-work-into-confluence/)

### ✏️ Real—time editing

You and up to 30 teammates can edit a page together - live - in real time. Changes save and sync automatically so that everyone editing sees the same thing.

### 📏 Tables

Tables allow you to organize important information on a page to discuss it with your team.

Present the information the way it makes sense to you—resize columns, color cells, rows and columns, and sort the table by clicking the column headers.

[More about tables](https://confluence.atlassian.com/conf73/tables-991927743.html)

### 📸 Header images and emojis

Make your pages more visually impactful with header images and emojis.

### **💬**Comments and mentions

Comments are a great way to start a conversation about a page or blog post. They allow you to remark on content, add important information, ask questions, and generally drive collaboration and teamwork.

@mention a teammate to bring them into the conversation, and they can reply to and/or like comments.

[More about comments](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/comment-on-pages-and-blog-posts/) | [More about mentions](https://support.atlassian.com/confluence-cloud/docs/mention-a-person-or-team/)
